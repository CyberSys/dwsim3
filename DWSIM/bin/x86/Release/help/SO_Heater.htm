Heater

The heater simulates a stream heating process.

Input Parameters

Pressure drop: pressure drop in the heater.
Efficiency: heater efficiency.
Calculation mode: depending on calculation mode a dedicated parameter is specified:

Mode Parameter
Heat added: Heat added
Outlet temperature: Outlet temperature
EnergyStream: Power for heating is taken from connected energy stream.
Phase molar fraction: Molar fraction of vapour phase

Calculation

The outlet stream temperature is calculated by doing a PH Flash, were the outlet stream enthalpy is calculated by a energy balance in the heater.

In calculation modes other than "EnergyStream" the transfered energy is written to the energy stream.

Output Parameters

Delta-T: Temperature rise observed in the heating process.
Heat added: Heat added to the process.
